The cliffs begin inconspicuously;
ridges, fractures in the plain. Then
they fold and fold over again, rising
above ever deeper arroyos, the pink
and blue pillows of rock rising to the
grooved plateau of the Blasted Field.
In cliff hollows, protected by filigree
lattices of living stone, spread the
Cliff Villages of the Citrus Ecclesia,
the broad church of the elders of
the Steppelanders, bound in the
circle of life and death. The common
elders are spry and industrious, but
eerily quiet and calm.
