The Limey Nomads’ lands are harsh and dry, forbidding to travellers.
Odd remnants from the misty period referred to as the
‘Best-Forgotten’ Ages by the Saffron City’s Opiate Priests dot the
plain. In spring, the Limeys graze west towards the Grass Colossus,
returning east to the Circle Rim for winter.
