After a week in the steppe, [Tifixa](../../directory/Atles/Tifixa.md) starts to feel very sick.
She knows the [iridescent globes](../../directory/FoldedBelow/IridescentGlobes.md) are causing it. 
Her eyes are all coloured and her skin's inside looks like a rainbow.
Her flesh hurts, especially on its folds.

Together with [Pilik](../../directory/Atles/Pilik.md) and [Rise Ghost](../../directory/Atles/RiseGhost.md), they leave their globes with [Vorgo](../../directory/Atles/Vorgo.md) and depart northeast, towards the lavender hills, just as some [Violet City](../../directory/VioletCity/index.md) detectives arrive in the [steppe](../../directory/LimeNomads/index.md).

[Atles’ Caravan](../../directory/Atles/index.md) stays behind.

## Towards the lavender cliffs

It is a long journey, and the lime-coloured grasslands turn bluish, and then there are some rose hues.
The smell of lavender fills the air.
A [lime](../../directory/LimeNomads/LimeClan.md) scout detachment approaches the group with caution.
They explain that the wolf-folk can be dangerous further north of the hills.

Tifixa shows some animal interest in the scout’s skin.
After the kerfuffle, she reminds herself of [Fleshy](../../directory/FoldedBelow/Skinwearer.md).
Is she turning into one of them?

Pilik can connect to Tifixa’s infection through his interface.
It proves his theory that the vomish infection is of a nanobot nature.
He turns them off, which stops her sickness.
For now.

## Lavender Cliffs’ villages

The group arrives at the [Lavender Cliffs](../../directory/LimeNomads/LavenderCliffs.md).
Small villages are scattered atop them, built upon ruins of a bygone civilisation.
In the centre of one of them sits an elder.

His eyes are milky, and he receives a communal spirit that he shares with the elders of other villages.
Except on Sundays, when he is free to be himself.

The group learns of the mountain city to the east.
It was probably a city, but not anymore.
Now, an Ultra lives there by herself.

## Travelling back

After some light trading, they decide to come back to the steppe.
A few days later, before arriving, they find themselves lost - but still in the right direction.
They cross a field scattered with obsidian remains of some ancient battle.

Rise Ghost finds a sword that is too large to be wielded by humans, but also light in weight and sharp.
Close to it, they find an equipment name-tag, where it can be read:  
***“Property of the Pre-Fundamentalists Utopian Ecstasics"***  
Tifixa and Pilik know about them.
