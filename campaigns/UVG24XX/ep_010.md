## A bigoted Prince

[Tam HR](../../directory/Atles/TamHR.md) takes the opportunity to offload the Chitin caps they picked up in the [Porcelain Citadel](../../directory/PorcelainCitadel/index.md).
It is a good profit.

While Tam is getting rich, [Kur Barista](../../directory/Atles/Barista.md) mingles with the high societies of the [Last Serai](../../directory/LastSerai/index.md).
The highest ranking Porcelain Prince in town, [Angel 22-unity](../../directory/LastSerai/Angel22-unity.md), befriends the barista.
The bigoted Prince "explains" why the Last Serai does not deserve the [Harmonium](../../directory/LastSerai/Harmonium.md), and their plan to move it to the Porcelain Citadel.

The plan hinges on predicting the Near Moon and entering the [Pit of Mould](../../directory/FoldedBelow/PitOfMould.md) to activate the transport through "unfolding".
Angel marks the Pit's location on the map.

## Doda! Now?

A few days later, in a grassland-driven hallucination, Tam sees her Dodo.
The bird is of violet colour and, more strangely, it is flying.
The Dodo lands gracefully and flexes its newly implanted solar-powered wings.
Tam names it Deep Purple.

Kur catches up, and they spot someone crawling toward the Last Serai.
Deep Purple is promised a Doda to fly past the crawler. The bird obliges gracefully, and returns with little information.

## Iron Pot

The crawler is one of the bodies of [Iron Pot 6-body](../../directory/LastSerai/IronPot6-body.md).
This body, and two others, were assailed by a slave — impeding their investigation into Angel 22-unity's revolutionary activities.
Tam uses their psychic band-aid to heal this Iron Pot body.

Kur — barista, but also a strategist (almost) — tells Angel of the investigation. Angel quickly requests help knocking down Iron Pot.
With help from Tam (and Deep Purple), they pretend to heal the other two bodies — but "accidentally" fix them wrong.

Something is cooking.
