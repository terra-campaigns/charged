## A bigoted Prince

[Tam HR](../../directory/Atles/TamHR.md) takes the opportunity to make their money selling the Chitin caps they picked up in the [Porcelain Citadel](../../directory/PorcelainCitadel/index.md).
It is a good profit.

While Tam is getting rich, [Kur Barista](../../directory/Atles/Barista.md) continues to mingle with the high societies of the [Last Serai](../../directory/LastSerai/index.md).
The highest ranking Porcelain Prince in town, [Angel 22-unity](../../directory/LastSerai/Angel22-unity.md), befriends the barista.
The bigot Prince "explains" why the Last Serai does not deserve having the [Harmonium](../../directory/LastSerai/Harmonium.md) here, and their plan to move it to the Porcelain Citadel.

The plan includes predicting the Near Moon and entering the [Pit of Mould](../../directory/FoldedBelow/PitOfMould.md) to somehow activate the transport through "unfolding".
Angel also points out the correct location of the Pit on the map.

## Doda! Now?

A few days later, in a grassland-driven hallucination, Tam sees her dodo.
The bird is of violet colour and, more strangely, it is flying.
The dodo lands gracefully and starts flexing their newly implanted solar-powered wings.
Tam decides to call the dodo Deep Purple from now on.

Kur catches up with them, and they all notice someone crawling in the direction of the Last Serai.
Deep Purple is promised a Doda if they fly past the crawling person, which the bird does gracefully (and returns with little information).

## Iron Pot

The crawling person is one of the bodies of [Iron Pot 6-body](../../directory/LastSerai/IronPot6-body.md).
This body, and two others, were assailed by a slave, impeding their investigation into the revolutionary activities of Angel 22-unity.
Tam uses their psychic band-aid to heal this Iron Pot body.

Kur, the barista, but also a strategist (almost), tells Angel of the investigation, and Angel quickly requests the services to knock down Iron Pot.
With the help of Tam (and Deep Purple), they pretend to heal the other two bodies, but "accidentally" fix them wrongly.

Something is cooking.
