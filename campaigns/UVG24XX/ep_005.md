A clique of [Atles](../../directory/Atles/index.md)' caravan leaves [The Low Road and The High](../../directory/LowRoadHigh/index.md) and travels towards the [Steppe of the Lime Nomads](../../directory/LimeNomads/index.md).
The travellers are [Tifixa](../../directory/Atles/Tifixa.md), [Kur](../../directory/Atles/Kur.md), [Pilik](../../directory/Atles/Pilik.md) and [Rise Ghost](../../directory/Atles/RiseGhost.md).

## Animals in peril

On departure, one of Tifixa's mules lames herself on a prairie dog hole.
The mule is ashamed of being so distracted.
A few days later, the mule's eyes' iris turns purple.

Halfway through the trail, a pack of prairie dogs chases the lame mule.
The mule tries to fight them off, but Tifixa, mounted on her warhorse, scares the dogs off.
The mule is badly hurt.

With vengeance in her eyes, Tifixa chases the dogs, who see themselves cornered by the warhorse.
They attack the horse, which kicks and bites the dogs as it can.
Tifixa falls from the horse, but is not hurt.
Pilik and Ghost run to Tifixa's help, and the canines are disbanded.

Ghost runs an unsuccessful crystal healing rite.
They bury the mule.
Before arriving at their destination, vultures circle above the caravan's head.
They are waiting for them to die.

## Spring of the yellow water

A group dressed in greenish-yellow robes runs a ceremony at the [spring](../../directory/LimeNomads/SpringYellow.md).
The leader of the [Lime clan](../../directory/LimeNomads/LimeClan.md) greets the group and invites them to participate.
The group chants in an ancient language, but the caravan archaeologists can understand a few words:

**biomechanical fish**  
**abyssossaurus**  
**sacred machine fetish** *or* **octopus** 

Ghost breathes too much of the spring vapours and falls into deep hallucination.
He sinks into the pool and sees fish, a friendly octopus tending to a garden, and a pile of treasure.
He then sinks into the treasure, and a nightmare kicks in.
A ceramic hand holds his hand, and a terrifying ceramic face scares him.

By the pool, the others see Ghost drowning in the hand-deep part of the pool.
In despair, the wizard pulls the cult leader into the pool.
It is Pilik who saves the man.

There are no hostilities between the two groups.
The leader respected Ghost's visions and mentioned that others had similar visions in the past.

The caravan continues its travel to a camp and settles for the night.
The main caravan should arrive in a few days.
