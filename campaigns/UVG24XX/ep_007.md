[Tifixa](../../directory/Atles/Tifixa.md)'s condition continues to deteriorate, while [[Kur]] start to show similar symptoms (milder for now).

A project manager, named Uele Xerive, joins the clique that will travel ahead of the main [Atles' Caravan](../../directory/Atles/index.md).
They will leave the [steppe](../../directory/LimeNomads/index.md) towards the [Porcelain Citadel](../../directory/PorcelainCitadel/index.md).

A few kilometers after departure the group is stoped by a trail block from [Violet City](../../directory/VioletCity/index.md)'s detectives.
They search for [Vorgo](../../directory/Atles/Vorgo.md), but Pilik and Kur point them to the [Crystal Pylon](../../directory/LowRoadHigh/CrystalPylon.md) instead.

The journey to the citadel is peaceful.
On the way they meet and hire a helpful wandering serai as an intern.

In the citadel they try to find treatment for Tifixa's condition.
However it only gets worse.
By the evening, all Porcelain Princes are observing as she climbs the hand atop the hill to dance.
They eventually get bored and leave.

Tifixa does not stop, and a mob of workshop personnel and general workers come with drinks and drugs.
The party goes night in, with the caravan clique leading the fun.
Most end up drunk, some are robbed.
But not Tifixa.

She holds on her fingers the recently found zipper on her chest, and in one movement undresses her skin.
A creature of rainbow colours and light emerge, the music gets louder, and she dances harder.
In the morning, only her old flesh can be found.
